ABOUT

AnimeToWatch is a simple list app to help keep the overwhelmed anime fan organized with what they are watching now and what they want to watch in the future.
Simply press a button add the name of the anime you'd like to watch.
You have the option to save the date you started when you enter the anime's name or update it later.
Once you finish that anime you can then update that you've finished. Cutting out all the need for spreadsheet or other not taking apps for tracking the show you love.
